I am Landen Ingerslev, a Computer Science student at the University of Massachusetts Boston (UMass Boston), where I am currently studying computer programming. My academic interests span cybersecurity, and high level programming. At the moment I am working on various projects with friends that span from discord bots to programming language compilers - I am very active on GitHub and most of my projects are available there. Beyond the classroom, I have participated in the National Honor Society. I plan to pursue a degree in Computer Science with a concentration in cybersecurity and software engineering.


Prior to beginning my college studies, I completed the Computer Science and Information Technology program at Old Colony Regional Vocational Technical High School, where I built a strong foundation in programming, networking, and cybersecurity. During my time there, I worked on numerous applied projects, including developing Java-based applications, and python educational resources to teach network vulnerabilities through controlled simulations. I also served as a tutor and mentor through the National Honor Society, helping students strengthen their programming logic and technical confidence. My early experiences combining teaching, system design, and cybersecurity experimentation shaped my passion for computer science.

Outside of academics and computer science I have a passion for bodybuilding and longboarding. I also enjoy watching movies and reading comic books.
